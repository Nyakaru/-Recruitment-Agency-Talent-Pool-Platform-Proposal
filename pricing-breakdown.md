## Recommended Phased Approach

The project is designed to be delivered in sequential phases, allowing for:
- **Iterative development** with regular feedback loops
- **Risk mitigation** through incremental delivery
- **Budget flexibility** - pause after any phase if needed
- **Early value delivery** - each phase delivers working functionality

**Minimum Viable Product:**
- **Phases 0-1** (10 weeks, KSh 1,616,000 / ~£9,790) delivers a functional recruitment platform with essential compliance tracking

**Full Compliance Solution:**
- **Phases 0-2** (18-20 weeks, KSh 3,168,000 / ~£19,200) provides comprehensive UKVI sponsor licence compliance management

**Complete Workforce Management Platform:**
- **Phases 0-3** (22-28 weeks, KSh 4,752,000 / ~£28,800) includes bank staff operations and commercial revenue model
